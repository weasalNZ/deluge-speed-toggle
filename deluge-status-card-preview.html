Step 1: Copy the card file

Copy deluge-status-card.js to your Home Assistant www folder:

/config/www/deluge-status-card.js

Step 2: Add to Lovelace resources

In Home Assistant, go to Settings ‚Üí Dashboards ‚Üí Resources and add:

Step 3: Add card to dashboard

Add this YAML to your Lovelace dashboard:

type: custom:deluge-status-card entity: switch.deluge_speed_toggle_localhost_8112_switch name: "My Deluge Server" show_speed: true show_torrents: true
